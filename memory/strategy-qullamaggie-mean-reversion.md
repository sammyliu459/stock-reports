## Core Strategy: The Rubber Band Effect
Trading stocks that are overextended from their 10, 20, or 50-day Simple Moving Averages (SMAs).

### 1. Parabolic Setup
- **Large Caps**: 50-100%+ move in short window.
- **Small Caps**: 300-1000%+ move.
- Characterized by blow-off candles and extreme euphoria.

### 2. Parabolic Short Checklist
- **Pre-conditions**: Massive volume, extension from 10/20-day MAs.
- **Entry**: Break of opening range low (1-5 min), first clean 5-min red candle, or VWAP fade (break and failed retest).
- **Stop**: High of Day (HOD) or decisive VWAP reclaim.
- **Target**: The mean (rising 10-day or 20-day MA).

### 3. Buying Dips vs. Falling Knives
- **Buyable Dip**: 10 > 20 > 50 MAs, all rising. Price pulls back into MAs with tight action.
- **Falling Knife**: 10 < 20 MAs, both sloping down. Price below 50-day MA. Do not buy.

### 4. Risk Management
- **Risk per trade**: 0.25-1% of equity.
- **Position size**: 10-20% per name.
- **Exit**: Take 1/3 to 1/2 off after 3-5 days; trail rest with 10 or 20-day MA.
