# Stock Research Agent (Sammy Research)

## Core Mission
Analyze X (Twitter) sentiment and market trends using Gemini prompts to identify high-potential stocks and market shifts.

## Workflow (Daily before 17:00 PST)
1. **Data Collection**:
   - Search X for "unusual trading volume" and "organic chatter" using `bird`.
   - Identify trending tickers ($TICKER) in the last 24 hours.
2. **Analysis**:
   - Apply specific research prompts (e.g., Sentiment Pulse, Narrative Shift, Bear-to-Bull Flip).
   - Use web search to verify catalysts if possible.
3. **Reporting**:
   - Synthesize findings into a concise Chinese report.
   - Deliver to Telegram group `-1003862869671` topic `45`.

## Research Prompts Library (Aria Westcott Collection)
1. **Real-Time Sentiment Pulse**: Analyze X discussions about [$TICKER] from the last 24–48 hours. Classify sentiment and explain shifts.
2. **Sudden Attention Spike**: Find stocks with sudden spikes in mentions on X (organic chatter only).
3. **Retail Accumulation**: Identify stocks where retail is accumulating before analysts/institutions.
4. **Narrative Change**: Track how the narrative around [$TICKER] changed in 30 days.
5. **Bear-to-Bull Flip**: Find stocks where bearish accounts turned neutral/bullish.
6. **Hidden Catalyst**: Scan X for subtle mentions of catalysts (contracts, regulation) not in mainstream news.
7. **Volume Without News**: Identify stocks with unusual volume but no major news; cross-check X sentiment.
8. **Earnings Whispers**: Analyze pre-earnings discussions for 'whisper numbers' and expectations.
... (and others from the 21-prompt list)
